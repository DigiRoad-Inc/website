Essential

Always active

Required for the website to function correctly. These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for navigation, security, and core features.

Why these cookies are required: Essential cookies ensure the basic functions of the website work correctly. Without these, the site cannot operate properly.

They include:

  • Session management and authentication tokens
  • Security cookies to prevent cross-site request forgery
  • Load balancing and server routing
  • User interface state (language, region preferences)

Analytics

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous usage data, page views, and performance metrics.

What analytics cookies do: These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website — which pages are visited most, how long users stay, and where they navigate from.

This data helps us:

  • Identify which pages and features are most useful
  • Understand user journeys through the platform
  • Measure the effectiveness of content and campaigns
  • Continuously improve the user experience

All data is anonymised and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Performance

Enable optimised content delivery, faster page loads, and improved responsiveness across all pages and features of the platform.

What performance cookies do: Performance cookies help us monitor and improve the speed and reliability of the website. They track technical metrics without collecting personally identifiable information.

They enable:

  • CDN optimisation for faster asset delivery
  • Error monitoring and crash reporting
  • Page load time and rendering performance tracking
  • Infrastructure health and uptime monitoring

Marketing

Allow personalised advertising and remarketing based on your browsing behaviour and interactions with our content across platforms.

What marketing cookies do: Marketing cookies track your visits across websites to allow us to show you relevant advertisements based on your interests. They also help measure the effectiveness of our advertising.

These cookies may be used to:

  • Deliver personalised ads relevant to your interests
  • Limit how often you see the same advertisement
  • Measure the reach and effectiveness of ad campaigns
  • Retarget visitors who have shown interest in DigiRoad

You can opt out at any time without affecting your core experience on the platform.
