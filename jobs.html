The opportunity

A rare chance to build infrastructure that matters

Universities manage the identity, payments, and digital lives of over 200 million students globally. Yet most of this infrastructure is fragmented, outdated, and disconnected.

DigiRoad is building the operating system for this ecosystem , a unified layer for identity, cross-border payments, and institutional digital services.

We are early, growing fast, and solving problems that have not been solved at scale before. The people who join now will shape the foundation.

Global scale from day one. Our infrastructure already spans 44 countries and 500+ institutions.

Real complexity, real impact. Payments, identity, compliance, and institutional operations , all in one platform.

An early but credible stage. Enough traction to validate the model, enough runway to move fast.

The work

Hard problems worth solving

Building infrastructure for institutions is genuinely difficult. Here is what makes the work at DigiRoad both challenging and meaningful.

Reliability at institutional scale

Universities depend on our infrastructure for enrolment, payments, and identity every single day. Downtime is not an option , we build for 99.99% uptime from the start.

Cross-border payments complexity

Moving money for students across 44 countries means navigating local schemes, regulatory frameworks, FX volatility, and compliance requirements simultaneously.

Identity for millions of students

Verifying and managing identities for students across institutions, countries, and credential types , while keeping systems simple for the people who use them every day.

Compliance without friction

PCI DSS, GDPR, local financial regulations , compliance must be deeply embedded in our architecture, not bolted on. We build this in from the beginning.

Products for two audiences

We build for institutions making strategic decisions and for students navigating daily life. Both must feel effortless. Serving both well requires deep product thinking.

API design for institutions

Building APIs that developers love and institutions trust , expressive, stable, versioned, and resilient. Our API is the product for many of our partners.
