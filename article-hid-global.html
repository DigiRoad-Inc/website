DigiRoad and HID Global Partner to Bring Mobile Credentials to Irish Universities, Transforming Student Experiences

DigiRoad partners with HID Global to introduce mobile credentials to Irish universities, enabling seamless student access, improved security, and a fully digital campus experience.

DigiRoad's eCampus mobile credential integrates with HID Origo™ readers across Irish university campuses.

The partnership enables Irish universities to adopt university-wide virtual ID cards with HID readers.

DigiRoad are thrilled to announce a groundbreaking partnership with HID Global, aimed at revolutionising the way Irish universities manage identification and enhance student experiences. This collaboration brings the exciting opportunity of mobile credentials to campuses across Ireland, enabling a new form of identification that will transform how students interact and navigate their day-to-day routines.

This innovative solution will empower students with a host of benefits. Firstly, mobile credentials eliminate the need for carrying multiple cards, reducing the risk of loss or damage. With a simple tap or scan on their smartphones, students will seamlessly gain access to classrooms, libraries, residence halls, and other campus areas. No longer will they have to fumble through wallets or worry about forgetting their IDs.

We are delighted to welcome DigiRoad to the HID Origo™ Technology Partner Program. DigiRoad's deep knowledge of the university landscape and their focus on the student experience is a wonderful complement to our Technology Partner Program. We look forward to working with multiple Irish universities, as well as expanding this partnership to other markets in the near future.

Matt Bennett Director of Strategic Alliances, HID

The collaboration between DigiRoad and HID also extends beyond convenience. By leveraging advanced security measures and encryption technologies, mobile credentials offer heightened protection against fraud and unauthorised access. Students can have peace of mind knowing that their personal information is safeguarded within their smartphones, strengthening overall campus security.

This partnership is poised to reshape the landscape of Irish universities, setting a new standard for student identification and engagement. It will enable students to seamlessly navigate campus, access services, and connect with their university community.

DigiRoad and HID are committed to delivering this cutting-edge solution to universities across Ireland, enabling a future where students can embrace the convenience, security, and flexibility of mobile credentials. Together, we are excited to embark on this journey of digital transformation in the education sector.
