The Process

How UniPay Works

01

Enrollment

Student completes KYC in under 3 minutes via biometric identity check. Virtual account and card issued instantly upon approval.

02

Funding

Student or sponsor initiates payment via any supported scheme , SWIFT, SEPA, UPI, PIX , in their home currency with transparent FX.

03

Execution

UniPay routes through the optimal payment corridor, applies real-time compliance screening, and settles in under 2 seconds.

04

Distribution

Funds land in the university's treasury account with full reconciliation data, triggering automated SIS and ERP updates.
